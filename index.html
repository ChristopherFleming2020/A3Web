Who we are

The GIS Pros team include geospatial experts that span a wide range of expertise and can help you make informed decisions based on solid GIS based evidence. We can help you realise your vision from start to finish. This includes identifying the base data needed to address your questions, developing an efficient and well designed workflow to explore and anlyse that date, and finally summarize and present the results in a way that everyone can understand.

We have experience working with a variety of stakeholders across all hierarchical levels. The application of GIS is wide reaching and transcend traditional divisions in sector type, research fields, etc. From independant companies, NGOs, city and government organisations, we have experience with all. For more detailed information on the service we offer and project we have completed, please refer to our Services page.
