The Joys of Spectral Imaregy

Remote Sensing allows us to detected objests from afar. Helpful for observing areas that many be out of reach.

An Imaregy Analysis Tool For Any Job

With Spectral imagery there is nothing that can't be analyzed. With use of orbital satellites, we have the ability to detect objects that are naked to the human eye, things such as soil moisture content, crop and forest health, the detection of acient ruins and many, many more.

Different imaregy analysis

From the Visible light spectrum we see everyday, and the rays on either side, it allows us to monitor global environmental health. The different frequency require different imagery bands to be observe, but once collect can be used together to show a unique display that can show detailed information.
